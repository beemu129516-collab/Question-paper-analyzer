# Question-paper-analyzer
The Question Paper Analyzer is an intelligent and user-friendly web-based application developed to simplify and automate the analysis of examination question papers. In traditional academic environments, both students and educators often spend a significant amount of time manually reviewing past question papers to identify important topics, understand exam patterns, and evaluate the difficulty level of questions. This process can be repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to human error. The Question Paper Analyzer addresses these challenges by providing a digital solution that delivers accurate insights quickly and efficiently.

The core functionality of the system revolves around uploading and processing question papers in various formats such as PDF, DOCX, and TXT. Once a file is uploaded, the application extracts the textual content and performs structured analysis using predefined logic. The extracted questions are categorized into relevant academic topics such as Operating Systems, Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Computer Networks, Cyber Security, Machine Learning, and Web Development. This categorization helps users understand which subjects are most frequently covered in exams.

In addition to topic classification, the system evaluates the difficulty level of each question. Questions are classified into three levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard. This classification is based on factors such as question complexity, keywords, and expected problem-solving effort. By doing so, the system provides users with a clear understanding of the overall difficulty distribution of a question paper, enabling better exam preparation strategies.

One of the key highlights of the application is its interactive dashboard, which presents analyzed data in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand format. The dashboard includes graphical representations such as bar charts and pie charts to display topic frequency and difficulty distribution. These visual insights allow users to quickly identify patterns, such as which topics appear most frequently or whether the exam leans more toward conceptual or advanced questions.

The application also includes a history management system, where all previously uploaded and analyzed question papers are stored. Users can search, filter, and sort these records based on parameters like upload date, file name, topic, or difficulty level. This feature makes it easy to track trends over time and compare multiple question papers, helping students and educators make informed decisions.

Another important aspect of the Question Paper Analyzer is its intuitive user interface. The platform is designed with simplicity and usability in mind, ensuring that even users with minimal technical knowledge can navigate and utilize its features effectively. Functionalities such as file search, quick preview, and deletion options further enhance the user experience.

Overall, the Question Paper Analyzer serves as a powerful academic tool that bridges the gap between raw data and meaningful insights. It not only saves time and effort but also enhances the quality of exam preparation by providing data-driven analysis. By identifying frequently asked topics and understanding difficulty trends, students can focus their studies more strategically, while educators can design better question papers and assessments.

In conclusion, this system transforms the traditional approach of question paper review into a modern, efficient, and intelligent process, making it an essential tool for today’s education ecosystem.
